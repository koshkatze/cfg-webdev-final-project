East Yorkshire

Whether you're looking for rolling hills, wild open spaces, calm beaches or rich wildlife, you'll be sure to find it within East Yorkshire. Choose from miles of walking paths dotted with cosy country pubs and spots to admire the views or cycle trails to venture off-road through amazing woodland.

Take a look into some of the most popular places to visit and the top things to do in East Yorkshire...

Walking

Whether you're looking for an easy morning stroll in the country or a full day of challenging hiking along rugged cliffs, East Yorkshire has something to suit the whole family. With miles of beautiful trails there's no shortage of routes to explore.

Flamborough Head

Home to the most spectacular chalk cliffs, Flamborough Head offers visitors dramatic cliff-top walks, spectacular sea views and has one of the most important seabird colonies in Europe.
