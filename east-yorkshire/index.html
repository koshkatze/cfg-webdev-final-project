East Yorkshire & Hull

Whether you're looking for rolling hills, wild open spaces, calm beaches or rich wildlife, you'll be sure to find it within East Yorkshire & Hull. Choose from miles of walking paths dotted with cosy country pubs and spots to admire the views or cycle trails to venture off-road through amazing woodland.


Take a look into some of the most popular places to visit and the top things to do in East Yorkshire & Hull...



Nature

Whether you're looking for an easy morning stroll in the country or a full day of challenging hiking along rugged cliffs, East Yorkshire has something to suit the whole family. With miles of beautiful trails there's no shortage of routes to explore.

Where to visit...

Flamborough Head

Home to the most spectacular chalk cliffs, Flamborough Head offers visitors dramatic cliff-top walks

Yorkshire Wolds Way

Explore the banks of the Humber estuary, woodlands, valleys and rolling hills where the view never ends

Wauldby Dam

Perfect in all seasons, surrounded by enchanting woodland, visit the photogenic lake with pin-sharp relfections

Shopping

Choose from your favourite fashion brands, independent shops offering the best in local produce, traditional butcher, bakers and producers or popular weekly markets. East Yorkshire & Hull offers a diverse shopping experience.

Where to visit...

St Stephens, Hull

Hull's most popular shopping destination that garuantees a stylish shopping experience

Flemingate, Beverley

Combining fashion, food, fun and film in Beverley town at the heart of East Yorkshire

Princes Quay, Hull

Boasting retail outlets, art galleries and fun for the family over Hull's Princes Dock

Food

A region full of delicious temptation and evocative aromas where our pubs, restaurants, delicatessens and cafes are bursting with amazing local produce. Discover award winning food and drink venues sure to be a hit with the whole family.

Where to visit...

The Pipe and Glass Inn

'Michelin-starred must visit' friendly pub located in Beverley, surrounded by idyllic Yorkshire countryside

Nibble

Exceptional breakfast and brunch made with quality local ingredients located within the thriving Hull Fruit Market

Whiteheads Fish & Chips

Award winning fish and chips named '2023 Uk Fish and Chip Shop of the year'located on the Hornsea coast

Entertainment

Home to a diverse range of entertainment venues, East Yorkshire & Hull host anything from major touring concerts, initimate gigs, theatre performances, stand-up comedy, family shows and sporting events.

Where to visit...

Bonus Arena

Stat-of-the-art music and events hosting up to 3,500 people. From major concerts to comedy and family shows

Bridlington Spa

Premier dance hall, theatre and conference venue with stunning sea views across the East Yorkshire coatline

Hull City Hall

Hull's most versatile venue showcasing some of the finest rock, pop, comedy, tribute bands and local choirs
